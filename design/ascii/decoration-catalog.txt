-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
AskeeDS Decorative ASCII Art Catalog
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Source of truth for named decorative ASCII art used with the
`decoration.placeholder` component in design/ascii/components.txt.

FORMAT
  - Entry start: line beginning with three U+241F (␟) then "ART: " followed by
    the art id, e.g. decoration.skull.small.
  - Meta: lines beginning with one ␟ then a key and value:
      ␟ title: Short human-readable title
      ␟ tags: Comma-separated tags (no spaces or with single spaces after commas)
      ␟ source: URL or "original"
      ␟ license: public-domain | CC0 | original
      ␟ notes: Optional; size or usage notes (e.g. 7x5, fits in card.simple)
  - ASCII art: everything after the meta block, until the next ␟␟␟ ART line,
    is the art block. Do not use the character ␟ inside art.
  - Size: Keep art within 80x24 overall; most pieces should be much smaller
    (e.g. <= 20x10) so they can fit inside cards and side panels.
